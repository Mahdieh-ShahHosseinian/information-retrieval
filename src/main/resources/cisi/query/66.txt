    Current online library network technology is described, including thephysical and functional aspects of networks.  Three types of networks aredistinguished:  search service (e.g., SDC, Lockheed), customized servicethat provide bibliographic files (e.g., OCLC, Inc., RLIN), and servicecenter (e.g., NELINET, INCOLSA).  It is predicted that as technologyevolves more services will be provided outside the library directly to theuser through his home or office.
