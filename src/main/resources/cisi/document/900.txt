  At one time or another many authors must have faced the dilemma ofwhether to gather their articles published on a certain topic and republishthem as a collection of essays or whether to rework them into an entirelynew book.  I decided in favor of the first course with regard to the articlesI had written during the last fifteen years on language and information,in particular on the more technical and applied aspects, leaving for somefuture occasion my papers on the philosophy of language.
