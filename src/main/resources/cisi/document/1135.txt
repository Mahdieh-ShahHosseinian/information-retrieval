   The essential differences between data retrieval system and documentretrieval systems are considered.. The notion of "fact" is discussed, analyzingthe influence of the definition adopted on the structure of a data retrievalsystem.. A proposition is advanced that a factographic JRS is a rudimentary butindispensable form on the way to a logical information system.. The latter typeof system by a capability for automatic analysis of input data and synthesis ofnew information.. The problem of the information retrieval language for dataretrieval system is discussed, as is its machine organization, intricately tied up with the specifics and functions of a system of that kind..
