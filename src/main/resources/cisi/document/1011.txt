  The authors of this article represent two specializations in thespectrum of information transfer activities, that of the computer andcommunication system engineer and that of the librarian.  These pointsof view are combined in examining the requirements for standardizationin the national efforts to use automation in publication, library,abstracting and indexing, and information-retrieval activities.Standardization is necessary to both the representation of informationand to the procedures being developed for handling it.
