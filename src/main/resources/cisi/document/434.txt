  This book has two objectives.  First, to reassess thoroughly the functionsof an indexing language or thesaurus in a information storage and retrievalsystem and in the light of this reassessment to analyze the structure of indexing languages and thesauri.  Most importantly, this reassessment is basedon a unified view of indexing languages (classification schemes) and thesauri as information storage and retrieval systems on the other.  It results in general principles that are applicable to a wide range of situations.
