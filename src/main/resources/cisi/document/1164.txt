In scientific process and primary scientific documents there are objectivecharacteristics, which allow the accurate and unambiguous reflection ofthe form and content of primary statements n the informational analysis oftheir secondary pattern.Using these characteristics allows one to develop a model of standardizedbibliographic and abstract document description and minimize subjectivity ininformation analysis.  The objective characteristics of form and content of documents and correspondingelements of bibliographic and abstractive description must be based onapplication of classification schemes.For each characteristic there must be a corresponding scheme of particularstructural complexity.
