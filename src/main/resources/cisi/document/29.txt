The expression "satisfying a requester's informationneed" is often used, but its meaning is obscure.  Theliterature on "information need" in relation toretrieval suggests three different (though not inconsistent)possible interpretations.  However, each of theseinterpretations is itself fundamentally unclear. Thevarious obscurities involved are indicated by criticalquestions, which those who write of information needare invited to answer.
