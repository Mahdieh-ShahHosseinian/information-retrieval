  This study's aim was to gather and analyzereliable data on the quantifiable characteristics of thebiomedical literature that affects communication problems withinthe research community.  Previous studies and standardbibliographic compilations were critically reviewed, particularlyfor data that could be used to determine changes with time.In addition, all publications generated during 1961-1962 bythe extramural and intramural research programs of theNational Institutes of Health (NIH) were analyzed as samplesof the current document output of US research.
