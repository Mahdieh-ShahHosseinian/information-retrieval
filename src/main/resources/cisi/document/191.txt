   ***MEDLEARN***, an orientation to MEDLINE, was developed to educate membersof the biomedical community to become competent on-line searchers.. It appearsin two complementary forms: an interactive terminal presentation available onthe TYMSHARE Network and a supporting hard-copy manual which contains thedidactic portions of the computer program as well as additional items which arenot suitable for on-line presentation.. ***MEDLEARN***, a flexible instructiontool, is divided into forty sections which may be selected in various sequencesdepending on the user's previous experience.. Two MEDLINE simulations andfeedback sections after practice on MEDLINE provide motivation for continuedlearning.. The orientation program was evaluated at several medical centerswith uniformly favorable results..
