   A synthesis of the second progress report submitted by the Library of Congress to the Council on Library Resources under a grant for the RECON Pilot Project.. An overview of the progress made from November 1969 to April 1970 in the following areas: production,Official Catalog comparison,format recognition,research titles, microfilming, investigation of input devices.. In addition, thestatus of the tasks assigned to the RECON Woking Task Force are briefly described..
