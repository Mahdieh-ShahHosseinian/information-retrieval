   Automated methods for selective dissemination of information (SDI) toindividual scientists and engineers play an important role in dealing with theincreasing avalanche of scientific information.. This article presents somebasic aspects of SDI systems and describes recent developments and problems..Two different approaches to indexing information for SDI systems are discussed,with emphasis on the desirability of using enumerative hierarchical classifications to improve the precision and quality of matching scientists with useful documents..
