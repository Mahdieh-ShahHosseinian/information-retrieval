   A recent article by Kozachkov and Khursin, entitled 'The basic probabilitydistribution in information flow systems', describes the fundamental similarityof a number of known statistical regularities in the flow of information.. Theypropose a basic model, called the 'hyperbolic ladder' and relate it, in particular, to work in linguistics by Zipf, in documentation by Bradford, andin the science of science by Lotka.. The purpose of this note is to speculate further on the relevance of Zipf's law in librarianship..
