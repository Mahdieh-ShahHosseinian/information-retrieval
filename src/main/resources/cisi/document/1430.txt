  A new step towards international co-operationin scientific and technical information.   The agencies specializing in documentation of documentation (i.e., handlingthe literature on scientific and technical information) are in turn havingproblems with the growth of information.  The number of their centres has been growing during the last few years - often several in a country and allwith activities that are often very similar; scanning and abstracting ofdocuments from the same sources, indexing jobs that may differ only inpractical details, more or less identical end-products (descriptivebibliographies, abstracting bulletins, indexes, selective distributionprogrammes, card-indexes, and so on).
