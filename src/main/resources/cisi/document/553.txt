        This book is an introduction to the field of information scienceand technology, with particular emphasis on document organization.Its point of view is general in the sense that it attempts to deal withtypes of problems rather than particular systems.  When particularsystems are described they are used as examples of systems ratherthan as models.        The method of presentation is expository at the introductorylevel.  While differing points of view are frequently noted, this bookis not intended to provide definitive critical evaluation.  Relevantresearch is reviewed in order to indicate current thinking and activitiesin the field.
