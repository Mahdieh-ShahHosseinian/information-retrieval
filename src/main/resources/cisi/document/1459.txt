  At the present time, about 15% of all the world publications of scientific and technical literature relate to chemistry and chemicaltechnology.  Each year throughout the world more than 250,000documents are published:  journal papers, specifications for authors'certificates and patents, scientific and technical reports, monographs,etc., and in the last twenty years the number of such documents hasincreased by an average of 9% a year.  In these scientific documentsinformation on 100-150 thousand new chemical compounds is published.
