  This report, a summary of the status of chemical information processing,is the latest in the series prepared by the National Academy of Sciences-National Research Council Committee on Chemical Information.  The periodcovered is 1969 to the present.  The user, technological developments,publications, services, the Federal government, academia, and industryare the topics reviewed as related to chemical information.  In additionto present status, trends are evaluated, problems are stated, and recommendations for action by appropriate bodies are included.
