  An International Serials Data System (ISDS) is being established withinthe framework of UNISIST - a world science information system.  Each serial will be assigned an International Standard Serial Number (ISSN).  An International Centre for the Registration of Serial Publications, withheadquarters in Paris, will be responsible for specifying the characteristicsof the world register and maintaining an up-to-date file of serial titles.National and regional centres will supply input on new titles to the registerand act as a link between the international centre and individual users.
