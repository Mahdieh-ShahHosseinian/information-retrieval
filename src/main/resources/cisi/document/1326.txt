    The automatic SMART document retrieval system was designed atHarvard University between 1961 and 1964, and has been operating of IBM7094 and 360 equipment both at Harvard and at Cornell University forseveral years.  The system takes documents and search requests in thenatural language, performs a fully automatic content analysis of thetexts using one of several dozen programmed language analysis methods,matches analyzed documents with analyzed search requests, and retrievesfor the user's attention those stored items believed to be most similarto the submitted queries.
