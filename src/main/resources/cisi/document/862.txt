   A synopsis of the third progress report on the RECON Pilot Projectsubmitted by the Library of Congress to the Council on Library Resources..An overview is given of the progress made from April through September 1970in the following areas:  RECON production, format recognition, researchtitles, microfilming, and investigation of input devices.  In addition, thestatus of the tasks assigned to the RECON Working Task Force are brieflydescribed..
