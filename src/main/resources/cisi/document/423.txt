   A review of the literature on R and D project selection and an analysis of interview data suggest that there is a lack of testing and use of the methodsproposed.. Several OR-MS methods are identified and their current status isindicated.. Three representative procedures are examined in some detail.. It isargued that both a lack of testing concerning feasibility and shortcomings ofthe models themselves, help to explain why the methods have not been used.. Some of these shortcomings are identified and discussed.. Implications for future research are presented..
