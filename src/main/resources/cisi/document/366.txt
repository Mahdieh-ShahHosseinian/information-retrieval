  Current budget strains on college and university librariesrequire a stepped-up search for operating economies.  Thetimely topic of economics of approval plans of the ThirdInternational Conference on Approval and Gathering Plansfor Large and Medium Size Academic Libraries, held at theRamada Inn, West Palm Beach, Florida, February 17-19,1971, has thus generated considerable interest.  The papers published here constitute, by and large, originalresearch in this area.  The importance of these contributionscan hardly be overestimated, since they will no doubt moldand influence collection development practices in the currentimprint area.
