  This book is meant to be an aid and reference workfor those people who are interested in the design ofinformation systems.  Such information systems aretypified by the traditional libraries, but the sameproblems of information processing, storage, andretrieval are present in many government andindustrial organizations in many places besides thelibrary.  This book provides an illustration of thetools, equipment, and methodology that might beapplied to those problems.
