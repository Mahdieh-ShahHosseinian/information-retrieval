  This volume is the second in a series of Annual Reviews of progress in thefield of Information Science and Technology.  Like its predecessor, it attempts to describe, compare, and evaluate the most significant work thathas been reported in the field during the past year.  The effort has beenundertaken in the belief that such taking stock of accomplishments providesa valuable service to the specialists in the information science field.
