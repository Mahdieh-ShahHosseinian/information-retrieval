   An outline of methods and summary of findings of a study of American,Britishand Overseas physicists.. This project was conducted by Aslib Research Department (acting for the Institution of Electrical Engineers) and the American Institute of Physics.. It took place in 1966, the first year ofpublication of Current Papers in Physics.. Aims were to assess the need for andreactions to this new current awareness journal and suggestions for improvement,and to obtain background information on the current awareness requirements of the physics community..
