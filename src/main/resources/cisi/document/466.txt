   An outline is given of the history of the Project and the development, with OSTI support, of an information service in intestinal absorption which is intendedto become self-supporting.. Results of an evaluation of computer-based current awareness techniques including journal scanning is discussed with reference to cost, completeness and minimum delay.. A simple technique is suggested forprofile construction e.g. for Chemical Titles computer search, based on frequency and specificity of words in a sample of relevant titles..
